My Internship in Logitech EE Team

Written by Lucile Yang with ♥ on October 2, 2021 in Uncategorized

Goal

Developed an improvement proposal for the Logitech G915 TKL keyboard based on brainstorming, cross-team discussions and user interviews

  • Look cool
  • Better user experience
  • Practicality

Mothod

Disassembled the model, added firmware codes, and modified the circuit board to build a prototype.

Timeline

What I’ve learned

  • Know the responsibilities of various departments of the company and relationships between them.
  • Know more about the process of product development
  • Know the importance of user needs and the market structure
  • Familiar with the working mode that requires cooperation between different departments
  • Review C/C++
  • Learn presentation skills
  • Practice communication and expressing ideas in English
